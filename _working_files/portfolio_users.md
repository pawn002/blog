### Target users to center when treating your portfolio like a product

Below is a focused set of user types you should design for. For each I include their primary goal, a measurable success metric, and one concrete design implication you can act on.

| **User** | **Primary goal** | **Success metric** | **Design implication** | **Priority** |
|---|---|---:|---|---:|

---

### Quick notes on using this list
- **Design for the highest priority users first** to maximize hiring outcomes and signal strength in Ethical AI and accessibility.
- **Surface evidence differently** for each user: visual story-driven cases for design leads, code and artifacts for engineers, outcome syntheses for managers and committees.
- **Make a single canonical narrative** that each user can consume at different depth levels: 30 second, 3 minute, and 15 minute pathways.
